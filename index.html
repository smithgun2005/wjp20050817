About

About Me个人简介

I am a junior undergraduate student at Wenzhou-Kean University, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematical Science. I have been working as an undergraduate research assistant at the International Frontier Interdisciplinary Research Institute, Wenzhou-Kean University since April 2025, under the guidance of Prof. Zhiqiang Gao. 我是温州肯恩大学数学科学专业本科三年级学生，目前攻读数学科学文学学士学位。自 2025 年 4 月起， 我在温州肯恩大学国际前沿交叉研究院担任本科生研究助理，师从高志强教授开展科研工作。

My recent work spans model editing and merging, generalization, representation learning, world models, self-supervised learning, and self-improving models. Four of my first-author papers have been accepted to main conference tracks, including AAAI 2026 and ICML 2026. 我的近期研究方向包括模型编辑与模型合并、泛化、表征学习、世界模型、自监督学习和自我改进模型。 目前已有 4 篇第一作者论文被会议主会接收，包括 AAAI 2026 和 ICML 2026。

Research

Research Interests研究兴趣

01

Model Editing and Merging模型编辑与模型合并

Difference vectors, task arithmetic, space alignment, and methods for reducing task interference. 研究差分向量、任务算术、空间对齐，以及降低任务干扰的模型合并方法。

02

Generalization and Representation泛化与表征学习

Optimization behavior, neural collapse, imbalance learning, and robust training objectives. 关注优化行为、神经坍缩、类别不均衡学习，以及更稳健的训练目标。

03

World Models and Self-Supervision世界模型与自监督学习

Current interests include world models, self-supervised learning, and self-improving model systems. 近期重点关注世界模型、自监督学习，以及可自我改进的模型系统。
